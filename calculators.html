What is TDEE?

Information pasted from Max Healthcare
Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) represents the overall caloric intake required by your body each day to maintain your current weight. This includes all the calories burned through daily activities, including basic bodily functions, physical activity, and the food's thermic effect (the energy required to digest and process food). Knowing your TDEE can assist you in weight management by offering valuable information on the caloric intake required to maintain, reduce, or increase your weight.

What is BMI?

Inforation pasted from CDC
BMI is a calculated measure of a person's body weight (in kilograms) divided by the square of their height (in meters). Although BMI does not directly measure body fat, BMI is moderately to strongly associated with other measures that capture the amount, location, and distribution of body fat.
