Recommended Workout Plan:

Build.

Perfect for anyone looking to build some muscle, this training programs follows the popular bodybuilding Push-Pull-Leg (PPL) split. Designed to maximize muscle growth and strength, this program will take you through a balanced mix of compound lifts and isolation exercises to target every major muscle group. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced lifter, this program can be tailored to your fitness level and be done 3 or 6 days a week.
